PERSONAL SAFETY DURING HAZARDS

SAFETY GEAR:
- Helmets, N95 masks, gloves, goggles, life jackets.

SAFE SHELTER:
- Earthquake: under sturdy furniture.
- Flood: high ground.
- Fire: outside building.
- Storms: storm shelters.

GO-BAG CHECKLIST:
- ID, documents, medications, food, water, flashlight, batteries, cash, charger.

MENTAL PREPAREDNESS:
- Stay calm, follow plan.

SITUATIONAL AWARENESS:
- Observe surroundings, anticipate danger.
