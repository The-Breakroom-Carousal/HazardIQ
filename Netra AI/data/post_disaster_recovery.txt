POST-DISASTER RECOVERY

RETURNING HOME:
- Check structural safety.
- Avoid damaged electrical lines.

SANITATION:
- Boil water, proper waste disposal.

HEALTH:
- Avoid stagnant water.
- Seek medical help for injuries.

PSYCHOLOGICAL FIRST AID:
- Listen, comfort, encourage.

INSURANCE & RELIEF:
- Document damage, contact authorities.

COMMUNITY HELP:
- Volunteer, donate, support others.
