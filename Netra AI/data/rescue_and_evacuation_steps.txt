RESCUE & EVACUATION GUIDELINES

FIRE EVACUATION:
- Stay low to avoid smoke.
- Use stairs, not elevators.
- Know exits.

WATER RESCUE:
- Use flotation devices.
- Avoid entering fast currents unless trained.

EARTHQUAKE SAFETY:
- Drop, Cover, Hold On.
- After shaking: exit to open area.

EVACUATING VULNERABLE GROUPS:
- Assist elderly, disabled, children first.

ROPE RESCUE BASICS:
- Learn basic knots, belaying techniques.

ESCAPE ROUTES:
- Mark in homes/offices, keep clear.

SIGNALING DURING RESCUE:
- Use whistles, flashlights, bright cloth.
