EMERGENCY CONTACTS & COMMUNICATION

REPORTING HAZARDS:
- State location, type, severity, people affected.

NUMBERS:
- Police: 100
- Fire: 101
- Ambulance: 102
- Disaster Helpline: 108 (India example)
- International: 112

RADIO FREQUENCIES:
- Keep battery-powered radio.

COORDINATION:
- Follow instructions from authorities.
- Use messaging apps if calls fail.

TECH USE:
- Emergency alert apps, GPS sharing.
